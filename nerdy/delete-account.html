Delete Account Request

Follow the steps below to permanently delete your Nerdy account.

Account Deletion Process

To delete your account, click the button below to send us an email. Your request will be processed within 30 days.

Data to be Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account information: Your username and email address
  • Game data: Your progress, scores, and completed cards
  • Achievements: All achievements and badges you've earned
  • Leaderboard: Your position in the rankings

Important: This action cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, it is not possible to recover your data.

Submit Request

Click the button below to delete your account. Your email app will open with a pre-filled template to submit your request.
