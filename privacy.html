Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

Math Snatch ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

When you use Math Snatch, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: If you sign in with Apple or create an account, we collect your display name and email address.
  • Game Data: Your progress, scores, completed cards, and achievement data.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, including features used and time spent.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app functionality
  • Track your learning progress and display statistics
  • Enable multiplayer features and leaderboards
  • Improve and optimize the app experience

Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using industry-standard cloud services. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.

Third-Party Services

Math Snatch may use third-party services that collect information. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Apple Sign In - for authentication
  • Firebase - for data storage and analytics

Children's Privacy

Math Snatch is designed to be educational and family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics collection

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
