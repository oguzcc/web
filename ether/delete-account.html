Delete Account Request

Follow the steps below to permanently delete your Ether account.

Account Deletion Process

To delete your account, click the button below to send us an email. Your request will be processed within 30 days.

Data to be Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and profile photo
  • Practice tracking data: All your spiritual practice entries and history
  • Meditation history and progress: Session logs and meditation statistics
  • Gratitude journal entries: All your journal entries and reflections
  • Community posts, stories, and comments: All content you created
  • Direct messages: All your private conversations
  • Habit tracking data: All habit logs and journey progress
  • Achievements and levels: All earned achievements and level progress
  • Subscription information: Your subscription status and preferences

Important: This action cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, it is not possible to recover your data.
