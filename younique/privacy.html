Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Younique ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Younique: Life Coach (the "App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account using Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Profile photo URL

Profile and Personal Data

Information you voluntarily provide to personalize your coaching experience:

  • Display name and bio
  • Profile image
  • Age, gender, height, and weight
  • Activity level and personal goals

Health and Fitness Data

Data you voluntarily record within the App:

  • Workout logs and exercise history
  • Steps, activity, and calories burned
  • Weight and body measurements
  • Progress photos (stored privately)

Nutrition Data

Data related to your nutrition tracking:

  • Logged meals and food items
  • Calorie and macronutrient intake
  • Water intake and dietary preferences

Habit Tracking Data

  • Habits you are building or breaking (e.g., quitting smoking)
  • Streaks, check-ins, and relapse logs
  • Reminders and goal progress

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Feature usage patterns and session duration
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique identifiers, and network status
  • Analytics Data: Firebase Analytics (anonymized)
  • Crash Reports: Firebase Crashlytics

Information Collected by Third Parties

Firebase (Google): We use Firebase for authentication, data storage, analytics, crash reporting, and push notifications. Firebase may collect authentication tokens, user identifiers, analytics data, crash reports, and push notification tokens.

Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

RevenueCat (In-App Purchases): When you make a purchase, RevenueCat collects transaction identifiers, subscription status, and purchase history.

RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Apple Health / Google Fit (Optional): If you grant permission, the App can read and write fitness data (such as steps, workouts, and weight) to integrate with your device's health platform. This data stays on your device and is not shared with us unless you choose to sync it.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Generate personalized workout, nutrition, and coaching plans
  • Track your fitness, nutrition, and habit progress
  • Provide tailored coaching tips and motivation
  • Send push notifications and reminders (opt-in only)
  • Process in-app purchases and manage subscriptions
  • Improve the App through anonymized analytics
  • Monitor app stability through crash reporting

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using:

  • Firebase Cloud Firestore: User profiles, fitness, nutrition, and habit data
  • Firebase Storage: Uploaded media (profile and progress photos)
  • Local Device Storage: Cached data and offline content

We implement appropriate security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and encryption at rest. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Your health, fitness, nutrition, and habit data is private to your account and is never shared publicly.

We may share your information with law enforcement or government authorities if required by law or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services.

Account deletion removes all personally identifiable information within 30 days.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account through the App's Settings or by emailing us
  • Notification Preferences: You can manage push notification settings through your device settings
  • Health Data Access: You can revoke Apple Health / Google Fit access at any time; related sync features will be unavailable
  • Camera Permission: You can deny camera access; profile and progress photo capture will be unavailable
  • Photo Library Permission: You can deny photo library access; selecting images from your library will be unavailable

8. Permissions We Request

  • Health Data (Apple Health / Google Fit): Optional, used to sync steps, workouts, and weight
  • Camera: Used for taking profile and progress photos
  • Photo Library: Used for selecting images for your profile and progress
  • Push Notifications: Optional, used for workout, meal, and habit reminders
  • Internet Access: Required for core app functionality

9. Children's Privacy

Younique is intended for users aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own through Firebase infrastructure. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using the App, you consent to such transfers.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
